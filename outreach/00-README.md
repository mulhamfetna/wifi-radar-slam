# Author outreach drafts

Personalized cold-contact drafts for the corresponding authors surfaced by the Round-1 literature
survey. Each `.md` file is one email: `To`, `Subject`, and body. Tone is peer-to-peer, specific to
that group's paper, and asks for something small and concrete (a preprint, a pointer, or openness to a
short call) rather than a large favor up front.

**Before sending:**
- Replace `[YOUR NAME]`, `[YOUR ROLE / INSTITUTION]`, and signature placeholders.
- Confirm every email address against the paper's own PDF (the arXiv-preprint addresses were not
  reliably extracted and are marked `CONFIRM`).
- Send individually, not as a group — these are personalized.
- Keep it short; researchers reply to short, specific, respectful emails.
