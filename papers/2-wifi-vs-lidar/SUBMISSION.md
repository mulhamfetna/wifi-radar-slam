## Honesty guards deliberately retained in the manuscript

These are *not* to be softened for submission — they are why the paper is defensible:
- Simulation-based; stated in the **abstract**, not buried in limitations.
- WiFi **matches** LiDAR; it does not beat it. No superiority claim.
- Cost advantage **inverts** with self-deployed APs (0.3–1.5×) — the sensitivity is reported.
- Fusion's **8× regression** (street/LiDAR-A) is reported, not tuned away.
- RQ2 is a **negative result**, reported with its mechanism.
- Budget 2-D LiDAR is a **price floor, not a peer**.
